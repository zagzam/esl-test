Vocabulary

Learn the new words for this lesson. 이번 과의 새로운 단어들을 배워보세요.

Name (이름)

Definition: The word people use to call you.

My name is Rina. 제 이름은 리나입니다.

My name is John. 제 이름은 존입니다.

Work (일)

Definition: Anything you do to earn money or get something done.

I work as a teacher. 저는 선생님으로 일해요.

I work as a chef. 저는 요리사로 일하고 있습니다.

Job (직업)

Definition: A specific position where you do work.

My job is teaching. 저는 가르치는 일을 합니다.

My job is driving. 제 직업은 운전입니다.

Hobby (취미)

Definition: An activity that you like doing when you are not working or studying.

My hobby is painting. 제 취미는 그림 그리기입니다.

My hobby is reading. 제 취미는 독서입니다.

저는 교사로 일하고 있습니다.

멋지군요! 취미가 무엇인가요?

Key Expressions

"What's your name?" 이름이 뭐예요?

How you can answer:

  • My name is name.
  • I am name.

Your turn:

"What's your name?"

"What do you do?" 무슨 일을 해요?

How you can answer:

  • I work as a job.
  • I’m a job.

Your turn:

"What do you do?"

"What is your hobby?" 취미가 뭐예요?

How you can answer:

  • I like to verb.
  • My hobby is verb-ing.

Your turn:

"What is your hobby?"

Reading

Read the passage below. 아래의 글을 읽으세요.

Hi! My name is Leo. I work as a singer. I love music. My hobby is singing. I also like running on Saturdays.

💡 Tip

You use 'Saturdays' (with an 's') to talk about something that happens regularly or every Saturday.

'Saturdays' (뒤에 's'가 붙은 형태)는 어떤 일이 정기적으로 또는 매주 토요일마다 일어난다는 것을 말할 때 사용해요.

Comprehension Check:

  1. What is his name?
  2. What is his job?
  3. What is his hobby?

1. His name is Leo.
2. He is a singer.
3. His hobbies are singing and running.
